about

SAFE stands for System for Anonymous Feedback and is an anonymous feedback system built to provide PSU students with a channel to contact staff. All feedback is sent to the department chair who forwards the feedback to the respective recipient.

Q.Will anyone know my identity?
A.No, Your identity is hidden to all users.
Q.How does the software work?
A.The software encrypts personal infomarion and sends just the message to the recipient.
Q.What should I use this for?
A.This software is designed to create a way to communicate to teachers or staff helpful information in a way that protects your identity.
